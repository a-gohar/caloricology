Instructions:

Before beginning logging food please fill out your current estimated daily energy expenditure (TDEE) and weekly weight change targets in kg. If you do not have an estimated energy expenditure, there are several calculators available online. To log a food, you must first supply a name for the food as well as caloric information per 100g. You may then repeatedly log it from the option menu by providing the amount of food consumed in grams. The website will keep a running average of your weight changes smoothed out to avoid daily fluctuations. If there is sufficient data in the last 3 weeks, it will provide an updated daily energy expenditure, otherwise it will fall back to the energy expenditure provided.
